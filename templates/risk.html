Job Description

We are seeking a seasoned Insider Risk Investigator to fortify our company, Singularity, against internal security risks and threats. In this job, you will play a crucial role in safeguarding Singularity’s assets by analyzing any irregular activities, promoting a secure culture, and with interacting with various departments to mitigate risks. Your expertise will be vital to protecting our company against potential risks, contributing to the broader societal benefits of artificial intelligence. In this role, you will correlate data from various sources and conduct investigations into suspicious activities in order to maintain security across the company and mitigate internal threats.

Job Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree/higher education in computer science and/or related subject
  • 3+ years of experience in cybersecurity or related domain
  • Mastery of SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) along with User Behavior Analytics tools
  • Strong communicative skills to be able to relay complex issues to non-technical stakeholders
